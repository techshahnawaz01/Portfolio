2. System Features & Specifications

Sales & Invoicing (Accounts Receivable)

  • Customers: Maintain basic client details and individual ledgers.
  • Estimates/Quotes: Send professional quotations convertible to invoices.
  • Invoicing: Generate professional tax invoices in PDF format.
  • Credit Notes: Issue credit notes for sales returns or discounts.
  • Payments Received: Track and record payments against issued invoices.

Purchases & Bills (Accounts Payable)

  • Vendors/Suppliers: Maintain supplier details and outstanding balances.
  • Bills: Record incoming purchase bills from suppliers.
  • Payments Made: Track payments made against supplier bills.
  • Expenses: Record daily operational expenses (petty cash, utilities).

VAT & Tax Management

  • Tax Setup: Define custom VAT/Tax rates (e.g., 5%, 15%, 0%).
  • Inclusive/Exclusive VAT: Dynamic tax calculation based on item price setup.
  • ZATCA/E-invoicing Support: Cryptographic QR Code and XML standard support for Middle-East compliance.
  • Consolidated VAT Report: One-click report showing VAT collected vs. VAT paid.

Core Accounting

  • Chart of Accounts: Define Asset, Liability, Equity, Income, and Expense heads.
  • Banking & Cash: Manage bank accounts and inter-bank transfers.
  • Journal Entries & Ledger: Manual accounting entries and individual ledgers.

Reports, Settings & Gateways

  • Essential Reports: Profit & Loss (P&L), Balance Sheet, Trial Balance, Customer/Vendor Statements.
  • System Settings: Company profile, financial settings (Currency, Timezone), Invoice prefix setup, and SMTP configuration for emails.
  • Payment Gateways: Integration with Stripe/PayPal/Razorpay for "Pay Now" links, plus manual offline methods (Cash, Transfer).
  • Security: Admin & Staff role management, Activity Logs, and Database Backups.
